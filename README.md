# Sales Analytics & Business Insights Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project is a complete business analytics case study built in Microsoft Excel using a retail Superstore dataset containing 51,000+ transactions.
The objective was to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, profitability trends, and future business opportunities using advanced Excel analytics techniques.


## Dataset

- 51,291 Orders
- 4 Years of Sales Data (2022–2025)
- 4,875 Customers
- Multiple Product Categories
- Regional Sales Coverage


## Business Questions Solved

- Which product categories generate the highest profit?
- Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- Which regions perform best?
- What sales can be expected in 2026?
- What strategic actions can improve growth?

## Key Insights

### Customer Segmentation
Champion customers generate a disproportionate share of revenue.

### Profitability
Technology products produce the highest overall profit contribution.

### Discounts
Higher discount levels negatively impact profitability.

### Growth Opportunity
Loyal customers present strong upselling and cross-selling potential.


## Business Recommendations

1. Increase retention programs for Champion customers.
2. Optimize discount policies.
3. Expand high-margin Technology products.
4. Target Loyal customers with premium offers.
5. Monitor low-profit regions for operational improvements.
